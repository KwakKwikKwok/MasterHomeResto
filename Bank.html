Mobile Banking:
  1. Open your BCA mobile and log in
  2. Select m-Transfer → Transfer Antar Rekening.
  3. Tap Daftar Transfer → Antar Rekening BCA.
  4. Input the destination account number: 872XXXXXXX
  5. Confirm and save.
  6. Go back to m-Transfer page
  7. Click Transfer Antar Rekening.
  8. Choose Destination Account
  9. Select the saved account (872xxxxxxx) from the list.
  10. Input Transfer Details with format [Name_DeliveryDate]
  11. Enter the amount to send
  12. Confirm the Transfer
  13. Review the details: recipient name, account number, and amount.
  14. Enter your m-BCA PIN to confirm.
  15. Screenshot payment confirmation
  16. Click done and upload payment confirmaion

Via TM:
  1. Insert your ATM card and enter your PIN.
  2. Select Transfer → To BCA Account
  3. Tap Daftar Transfer → Antar Rekening BCA.
  4. Enter the recipient's account number [872XXXXXXX] and amount.
  5. Verify and confirm the transaction.
  6. Collect the printed receipt.
  7. Take a picture of the receipt
  8. Click done and upload payment confirmaion
